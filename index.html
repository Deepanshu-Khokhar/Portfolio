Data Visualization

Digital Divide in Indian Schools: A Data Analysis This report explores the disparities in IT infrastructure across Indian schools using a government dataset. Analyzing the availability of digital resources in government, private, aided, and unaided schools, the findings highlight how rural schools lack proper IT facilities and trained teachers, putting students at a disadvantage in today’s technology-driven world. Using Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, I cleaned, visualized, and interpreted the data to uncover key insights. The analysis underscores the urgent need for investment in digital education to ensure that students from all backgrounds have equal opportunities to thrive in the modern workforce. This report aims to spark discussions on bridging the digital divide and improving digital literacy for future generations.
